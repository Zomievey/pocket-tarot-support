Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How do I use the Card of the Day feature?
    A: Simply open the app, and tap on the "Card of the Day" button to reveal your daily tarot reading.
  • Q: How do I perform a multi-card reading?
    A: You can perform a three- or five-card reading by selecting the desired spread from the main menu. Tap on the cards to flip them over and reveal your tarot insights.
  • Q: What do reversed cards mean in a reading?
    A: Reversed cards in tarot provide an alternative meaning to the upright version. Pocket Tarot includes reversed meanings for all cards to give you deeper insights.
  • Q: Can I learn about tarot cards through the app?
    A: Yes! Pocket Tarot provides detailed descriptions for each card, including its symbolism, upright, and reversed meanings, helping both beginners and advanced users.
  • Q: How can I customize my deck in the app?
    A: You can personalize your tarot deck by choosing different card designs in the settings menu. Select from a variety of card backs and themes.
  • Q: Do I need internet access to use the app?
    A: No, Pocket Tarot works offline, allowing you to perform readings and access the card descriptions anytime, anywhere.
  • Q: Will my past readings be saved?
    A: Currently, readings are not saved, but this feature is coming soon! You'll be able to log and review past readings for reflection.
  • Q: How do I reset or clear my reading?
    A: To reset a reading, simply navigate back to the main menu and start a new reading. This will clear the current cards and allow for a fresh draw.
  • Q: Can I share my tarot readings with friends?
    A: This feature is coming soon! Soon, you'll be able to share your readings directly from the app via text, email, or social media.
  • Q: I’m a beginner—how do I get started with tarot?
    A: Start with the "Card of the Day" feature and use the detailed descriptions provided for each card to learn their meanings over time.
  • Q: What’s the difference between Major and Minor Arcana cards?
    A: Major Arcana cards represent significant life events or spiritual lessons, while Minor Arcana cards focus on everyday situations and emotions.
  • Q: Can I customize the spreads I use?
    A: Pocket Tarot currently offers pre-designed spreads, including one-card, three-card, and five-card readings. Custom spreads may be added in future updates.
  • Q: What devices are compatible with Pocket Tarot?
    A: Pocket Tarot is compatible with iOS devices, including iPhone and iPad, and supports tablet use for a more immersive experience.
