Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How do I use the Card of the Day feature?
    A: Simply open the app, and tap on the "Card of the Day" button to reveal your daily tarot reading.
  • Q: How do I reset the app?
    A: You can reset your app by going to Settings > Reset.
